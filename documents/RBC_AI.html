            Artificial intelligence at RBC

            Artificial intelligence at RBC

            Learn how RBC is using artificial intelligence (AI) to build new capabilities and opportunities for employees and clients.

            Combined with powerful ideas, AI has the potential to change what a bank is capable of and enable us to make things better for you tomorrow than we did today.

            With a 150-year legacy of trust, ethical and responsible AI is core to our business. RBC’s existing business processes are designed to focus on the fair treatment of clients - ensuring that the use of AI respects human integrity, enhances wellbeing and achieves our purpose of helping clients thrive and communities prosper.

            Responsible AI principles

            Privacy & Security

            Privacy & Security

            At RBC, we are committed to responsible data practices – from how we use data to how we protect it. We maintain data integrity and confidentiality through robust information security and data handling practices.

            Accountability

            Accountability

            We follow protocols to ensure that AI systems are compliant with industry standards and regulatory guidelines. All AI systems must meet requirements throughout the development lifecycle, including in testing, validation, and monitoring.

            Fairness

            Fairness

            Our AI systems must uphold RBC’s core values of respect and integrity and mitigate unfair biases. To uphold these core values, RBC tests for fairness and strives to continuously improve models.

            Transparency & Responsible Disclosure

            Transparency & Responsible Disclosure

            We want our clients and stakeholders to understand how and when we use AI. We seek to provide relevant information so that those affected by the outcome of an AI system can understand the factors that led to a decision.

            Identity verification

            RBC is the first Canadian bank to introduce digital government identity verification solutions using AI-enabled technology, providing clients with more secure identity verification, stronger fraud protection and a faster account opening experience, from wherever it is most convenient for them.

            The power of generative AI

            RBC is focused on providing an exceptional client experience by using the power of generative AI to provide a more comprehensive view of their banking needs now and into the future. With a greater understanding of our client needs, we are in an optimal position to create the products and services that can make a difference.

            Electronic Trading

            A first for the industry, Aiden is a revolutionary AI electronic trading platform using deep reinforcement learning and advanced AI algorithms to learn and adapt to changing market conditions in real time.

            Learn more

            Data & AI insights

            The RBC Enterprise Data & AI Platform enables us to leverage our data scale to deliver differentiated solutions and unlock value for our clients and employees. The platform empowers RBC to safely transform data into trusted and high-value insights across our businesses and functions.

            Software development

            We’re empowering our 6,000+ developers through a centralized developer platform that automates build, test and deployment pipelines including AI-powered code generation. This platform enhances the software development workflow, helps address bugs proactively, improves issue resolution and enables us to stay ahead of evolving security threats.

            Employee support tools

            RBC employees have access to their own personal tech assistant - a guided AI-based tech support tool that helps troubleshoot and quickly resolve common technology issues.

            Advancing AI in finance - RBC Borealis

            Through RBC Borealis, RBC's dedicated AI research centre, we're leveraging the power of AI to better understand our clients, deliver personalized experiences and drive more efficient and effective operations.

            Investing in the AI community

            We believe being a world leader in artificial intelligence requires us to work together as a community to bolster and grow the AI ecosystem across the globe. Through our partnerships and by creating opportunities for homegrown IP, we’re a leading voice in the study of AI and are proud to see Canada as a top destination.
